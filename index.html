Light Up Your World with EmberLane

1. Dream Business

Name: EmberLane (Light Up Your World with EmberLane)

Our goal is to create a global e-commerce platform similar to Amazon or eBay, offering a world-class online shopping experience.

2. Problem Solving

We will provide top-notch customer support as our first priority. Our focus will be on ensuring customers are satisfied, followed by supporting sellers.

3. Facilities and Services

  • Consumers can create seller accounts and sell products on EmberLane to earn a living.
  • We will offer a wide variety of products including food, grocery, fashion, health, education, and electronics & beside this we will also be providing tech services like: Web Development, It services, etc.
  • To attract customers, we will offer products at lower profit margins, making shopping more affordable.
  • We will directly purchase from wholesalers or factories to get better prices, or allow sellers to join our platform and only take a 10% commission on profits.
  • We will open small physical stores in major cities, where customers can also buy products in person.
  • We will provide the option for people to open physical stores through our platform, featuring our branches.

4. Target Audience

  • We will be mainly targeting big and advanced tech cities in which most of the peoples are almost busy in their work, jobs, offices like the peoples who don`t have a time for going to retail stores physically and they mostly prefer to do online shopping and in major cities we will be providing delivery within one-two day so they can get products in fast & furious service.
  • Children (ages 4-12) who are interested in toys, fashion items, candies, and more.
  • Young females who are interested in cosmetics, jewelry, clothing and fashion items.
  • To attract the public, we will offer discounts, free items, and run ads on social media platforms.

5. Revenue Model

  • Commission from sellers.
  • Feature products from sellers for an additional fee.
  • Selling our own products directly.
  • Revenue from physical stores.
  • Revenue from It services by providing them webs apps ai resources.

6. Team

Initially, the team will consist of three main members:

  • One member for designing and digital marketing.
  • One member to manage business relations, seller approvals, and collaborations.
  • One member for revenue management, overseeing physical store operations.

7. Vision

  • In the next five years, EmberLane aims to expand globally.
  • We will be competing with other major e-commerce businesses.
  • Our services will grow, and our team will expand to ensure faster customer support and improved services.
  • By the end of five years, EmberLane will compete with global giants like eBay, Daraz in Pakistan, and Temu.
